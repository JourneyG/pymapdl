ansys.mapdl.core.Mapdl.lcsl¶

Mapdl.lcsl(nl1='', nl2='', nl3='', nl4='', nl5='', nl6='', nl7='', nl8='', nl9='', **kwargs)¶

Divides intersecting lines at their point(s) of intersection.

APDL Command: LCSL

Parameters

  • nl1 – Numbers of lines to be intersected. If NL1 = ALL, NL2 to NL9 are ignored and the intersection of all selected lines is found. If NL1 = P, use graphical picking to specify lines (NL2 to NL9 are ignored).

  • nl2 – Numbers of lines to be intersected. If NL1 = ALL, NL2 to NL9 are ignored and the intersection of all selected lines is found. If NL1 = P, use graphical picking to specify lines (NL2 to NL9 are ignored).

  • nl3 – Numbers of lines to be intersected. If NL1 = ALL, NL2 to NL9 are ignored and the intersection of all selected lines is found. If NL1 = P, use graphical picking to specify lines (NL2 to NL9 are ignored).

  • . () – Numbers of lines to be intersected. If NL1 = ALL, NL2 to NL9 are ignored and the intersection of all selected lines is found. If NL1 = P, use graphical picking to specify lines (NL2 to NL9 are ignored).

  • nl9 – Numbers of lines to be intersected. If NL1 = ALL, NL2 to NL9 are ignored and the intersection of all selected lines is found. If NL1 = P, use graphical picking to specify lines (NL2 to NL9 are ignored).

Notes

Divides intersecting (classifies) lines at their point(s) of intersection. The original lines (and their corresponding keypoint(s)) will be deleted by default. See the BOPTN command for the options available to Boolean operations. Element attributes and solid model boundary conditions assigned to the original entities will not be transferred to the new entities generated.
